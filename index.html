How It Works

  1. Sign Up or Log In: Create an account or log in for a personalized experience and to submit reports.
  2. Explore the Metro Map: Access live metro status, routes, and station details on the interactive map.
  3. Report an Issue: Click on the report button to submit safety, cleanliness, or technical issues at any station.
  4. Receive Alerts: Stay updated with real-time alerts and notifications about metro disruptions or emergencies.
  5. Track Your Reports: Monitor the status of your submitted reports and see how they contribute to a safer metro system.
  6. Community Engagement: Join discussions, share feedback, and help improve the metro experience for everyone.
